Aurora Brewing Challenge 2018

Please Read Carefully - New Judging Format

In order to accomodate an increasing number of entries, the ABC has asked the BJCP to allow us to try out a new judging format. They agreed and would like feedback from both judges and entrants once the competition has concluded.


What will change

  • You will only receive one scoresheet per entry
  • No more contradictory or repetetive feedback

    • What will not change

  • Your scoresheet will contain detailed feedback
  • Your entry will be judged by one or more qualified BJCP judges
  • You will still receive a numerical score for each entry

    • How this will work

    While the number of judges per flight will not change, we're adding the position of a scribe. The judges will discuss the entry while the scribe takes them through the sheet and records the information. We're hoping that this will reduce judge fatigue as well as reducing the amount of contradictory or repetetive information you receive as feedback. We're also expecting that talking through the evaluation process will be useful for less experienced judges, as well as for scribes who may be thinking about becoming BCJP qualified themselves.

    RULES

  • You must be 18 or older.
  • All entries must be received at Alley Kat Brewery before Thursday, May 24th at 6:00. No accomodations will be made for this as we are sorting at 6:00 p.m.
  • We can't judge broken or missing entries
    • If you work for a brewery, you still have to brew it at home with your own stuff. No, I won't believe you own a centrifuge at home.Â 
  • You have to make your beer with your own equipment
  • We'll group styles if we don't have enough entries in one category
  • If your beer is labelled incorrectly, that's not our fault
  • Best of Show will be judged on the second bottle. If there's no second bottle, it can't be considered for BOS

    • Other stuff


  • New England IPAs should be entered into the Specialty IPA category. Guidelines are here.
  • Milkshake IPAs go in Experimental
  • We're not picky about what you ship your entries in, as long as it's not absurd
  • Tape the label to your bottle with clear tape. Only the stewards will see it and they need to be able to read it.
  • Winners will be announced at the ABC gala on June 2nd. Tickets go on sale April 1st
