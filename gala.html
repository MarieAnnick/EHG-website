Edmonton Homebrewers Gala 2018

This year's Edmonton Hombrewers Gala will be held at Elmwood Community Hall.

The bar opens at 6:00 on June 2nd and catering will be provided by The Cookshack, featuring slow cooked, locally sourced brisket, pulled pork, BBQ chicken and Mac n' Cheese with vegetarian options.

$40 gets you all this plus drinks from some of Alberta's best local breweries including Blindman, Bent Stick Brewing, Blind Enthusiasm, Dog Island Brewing, Town Square Brewing, Village Brewery, Yellowhead Brewing Co. and Alley Kat as well as a cask off by mystery brewers! Which will win; classic West Coast IPA or upstart New England IPA?

The festivities end with the announcing of the winners of this year's Aurora Brewing Challenge, including Best of Show and New Brewer of the Year.
